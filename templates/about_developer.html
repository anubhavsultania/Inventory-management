About This Project

This Retail Inventory Management System was developed as part of the Database Management System course (CS2082) under the guidance of Dr. S. Asha.

The system provides a comprehensive solution for retail inventory management, featuring:

  • Real-time inventory tracking
  • Stock level monitoring with low stock alerts
  • Stock adjustment capabilities
  • Detailed stock movement history
  • Item management with unique SKU identification

This project demonstrates the practical application of database design concepts and web development technologies in solving real-world business problems.
